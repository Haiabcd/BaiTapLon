RETURN POLICY

Applicable policy: Apply 01 time to change 01 order.


- Original price products can be exchanged for other full-priced

products in stock. The customer pays the difference if the value of

the exchanged product is greater. No refund of change of any kind.


- Products with discount up to 30% can be changed color/size (if in

stock) or according to the regulations of each program (if any)


- Does not support changing to another product.


- Products not applicable for exchange include: Accessories,

underwear, products with discounts of 50% or more.


- Products can only be exchanged once.

Product exchange conditions


- Exchange within 03 days from the date the customer receives the

product


- The product is in its original condition upon receipt, with tags

and labels intact.


- The product has not been laundered or dirty, damaged.

II/ Payment Regulations.

Applicable policy:


- Customers are entitled to return products in case of defects

arising from the manufacturer and have no need to change to

another product.


- Cases of manufacturer defects include: color fading, material

defects, etc.


- Refund the defective product via bank account.


- Pantio bears 100% of the return shipping costs.


- Pantio will process within 10 days of receiving the defective

product.

Product return conditions


Return the product within 5 days of receiving the product.
